# Gazed
It is a first-person narrative puzzle game where players explore a room filled with personal objects. The core mechanic is scanning items to unlock fragmented text entries, which piece together a complete story when all clues are collected.

Youtube Link: https://youtu.be/tH6kYgrZWM4


https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8dde4ade-386f-4726-abb5-8259027e1804


Players move around the room (WASD controls) and interact with objects by focusing on them. A "Reading..." progress bar appears, indicating the item is being scanned to reveal its text clue. This mechanic encourages careful observation of the environment, as even small details like framed photos hold critical story fragments

All scanned text entries are automatically compiled into a Clue Notebook (opened with Tab). Each entry is paired with a photo of the object, making it easy to review and
connect the fragments. The notebook serves as both a progress tracker and a narrative archive, helping players piece together the full story as they collect more clues
